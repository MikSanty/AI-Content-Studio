## Product Details and Pricing (2024-2025) with Sources

### Relume
- **Pricing**: Free (30 Webflow components, 1 project), Starter ($32/month annual), Pro ($40/month annual)
- **Key Features**: 1000+ human-designed components, AI-powered wireframing, Figma & Webflow integration
- **Latest Updates**: Wireframing 2.0, Copywriting 1.5, 30 new pricing components, React Library v0.4.0
- **Source**: https://www.relume.io/whats-new/september-2025-release

### Figma
- **Pricing**: Free tier available, Professional ($12/editor/month), Organization ($45/editor/month)
- **2025 Updates**: Sites (website publishing), Make (AI prompt-to-code), Draw (illustration tools), Buzz (marketing content), Grid 2.0
- **Key Features**: Real-time collaboration, component libraries, advanced prototyping, AI-powered features
- **Source**: https://www.figma.com/blog/config-2025-press-release/

### Webflow
- **Pricing**: Free, Basic ($14/month), CMS ($23/month), Business ($39/month), Enterprise (custom)
- **Add-ons**: Optimize ($299/month), Analyze ($9/month), Localization ($9/month)
- **Key Features**: Visual development, CMS, responsive design, hosting included
- **Source**: https://www.foursets.com/blog/webflow-pricing-explained

### Framer
- **2025 Pricing**: Basic ($10/month), Pro ($30/month), Scale ($100/month)
- **Key Features**: Interactive prototyping, no-code development, real-time collaboration
- **Updates**: Simplified pricing structure, 24-hour email support on all plans
- **Source**: https://joechiang.com/blog/framer-new-pricing-plans-2025

### Swipe Pages
- **Pricing**: Startup ($29-39/month), Marketer ($59-89/month), Agency ($119-199/month)
- **Key Features**: AMP support, A/B testing, unlimited landing pages, multi-step forms
- **Source**: https://www.g2.com/products/swipe-pages/pricing

### Wix
- **2025 Pricing**: Free, Light ($17/month), Core ($29/month), Business ($36/month), Business Elite ($159/month)
- **Key Features**: Drag-and-drop builder, AI website builder, ecommerce capabilities, extensive app market
- **Source**: https://www.webdew.com/blog/wix/pricing

### Carrd
- **Pricing**: Pro Lite ($9/year), Pro Standard ($19/year), Pro Plus ($49/year)
- **Key Features**: Simple one-page sites, custom domains, forms and widgets (higher tiers)
- **Source**: https://landingi.com/carrd/pricing/

### Squarespace
- **2025 Pricing**: Basic ($16/month), Core ($23/month), Plus ($33/month), Advanced ($52/month)
- **Updates**: Start selling on any plan, lower transaction fees, expanded content features
- **Key Features**: Award-winning templates, built-in SEO, ecommerce capabilities
- **Source**: https://www.squarespace.com/blog/squarespace-plans-explained

## Common Objections to Address

1. **"These tools are too expensive for small teams"** - Address with free tiers, annual discounts, and ROI calculations
2. **"Learning curve is too steep"** - Highlight user-friendly options and available training resources
3. **"Our current tool works fine"** - Compare limitations vs. new capabilities and efficiency gains
4. **"We don't need all these features"** - Explain modular pricing and feature selection options

## SaaS Landing Page Best Practices Integration

### Essential Elements:
- Hero section with clear value proposition
- Social proof and testimonials
- Interactive product demos
- Clear call-to-action buttons
- Features/benefits sections
- Contact forms for lead capture

### Conversion Optimization Tactics:
- Simplify messaging and design for clarity
- Use compelling, unique copy that differentiates
- Implement interactive elements and animations
- Optimize for mobile-first experience
- Include video content and live chat integration
- A/B test all major page elements continuously
